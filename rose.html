This is not just a career recap.

Script Opening

Derrick Rose didn’t just lose games. He lost the future people had already imagined for him.

At 22 years old, he was the youngest MVP in NBA history. Chicago had hope again. Defenders were afraid of his first step before he even made a move. And for one short moment, it felt like the league had found its next face.

But the cruelest part of Derrick Rose’s story isn’t that he got hurt.

It’s that we saw just enough to know how great he could have been.

We saw the speed. The violence. The impossible finishes. We saw a player who attacked the rim like gravity was only a suggestion. We saw a city believe again.

And then, almost overnight, the story changed.

This is not the story of a player who failed. This is the story of a player whose prime was taken before it ever fully arrived.

This is the story of the Rose that never bloomed.
